## Strategy

Fixed strategy number, sorted by (x, y, z) ascending.

## Buy (x)

Entry band multiplier.
Determines how high the price must rise to trigger a buy signal.

## Sell (y)

Exit band multiplier.
Determines how low the price must fall to trigger a sell signal.

## Window (z)

Bollinger Band window length in days.
Determines the period for moving average and standard deviation calculations.

## Strategy Heatmap

Compare strategies by KPIs.
Click a cell to select a strategy and update the visuals.

## Equity Curve

Growth of the portfolio equity indexed to start from 1.
Index = 10 means the final equity is 10x compared to the initial equity.

## Drawdown

Peak-to-trough decline in portfolio equity.
Drawdown = -20 means the portfolio equity has dropped by 20% from its peak.
